// Known Intelligence

Inquisitor Corina commands an elite retinue aboard the vessel Ascendent. Her Ordo affiliation is unconfirmed. She has shown capability in ship-to-ship boarding operations, deploying Breacher teams and a Tome-Skull retinue with discipline and speed.

She successfully purged Chaos cultists from the Enginarium during the boarding action, securing critical junctions and allowing the Ascendent to complete its cleansing rites. However, the vessel translated into the Warp at Mandeville point under unknown conditions — every psyker aboard began to scream simultaneously. Corina's current status is unconfirmed.
